Art of Light brings green energy to schools

Art of Light is a school promotion programme that promotes environmental protection and energy saving to local schools through design thinking sharing sessions, artist-guided tours, competitions and exhibitions. Kindergartens, primary schools, secondary schools, colleges and the general public were all welcome to join this rewarding and meaningful programme. As part of the programme, the theme focused on “Low-Carbon and Emission Reduction & Encouraging Eco-Friendly Lifestyle”. My role was to design promotional products, including web, graphic, publication and exhibition design.

Developing main image

While colleagues were striving for carrying out the projects, I was in charge of the design works. In this project, the constraint of designing main image is that the light bulb have to be center to keep consistency of the programme image throughout the years. And by discussing with different teachers and colleagues, an earth tone is applied to project in response to the year theme and different from the previous years. Light beams are presented in layers and radial lines. Works of participants are also used on the lightbulb and layers to enhance the sense of participation.
