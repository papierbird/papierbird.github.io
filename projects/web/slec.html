Features of the platform

The online platform is consisted of news, booklist, link, powerpoint, video and assignment. It is designed with simple shapes, lines and features of a radio.

Online resources

Online learning materials, such as booklists, are prepared from books to digital format with some information. And as a blended learning course, these online resources could facilitate students learning at their own pace.
